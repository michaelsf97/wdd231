History of Trycicle

The trycicle is a three-wheel machine who helps people in short distances. Our history is beginning to be written in the past when the owner Michael Smith Saavedra Forero is the owner, when he was a child his parents purchased a trycicle when he rode that too many times. yes, too many times, and when he the first time needed to go to a urgency to the bathroom, he tried to take a bus but it was almost 20 minutes ballpark. And it cost sorrounding $3.50 USD. Then, i bear in mind to take a transmilenio which was largest transport here, then, it seems almost 30 minutes ballpark due to then to get out he would need to walk a little bit. Then a taxi, but it takes too much time. Finally, an informal trycicle driver offered him a route to, and then, he arrived in 10 minutes ballpark in front of his house and then it was shortest distance by 5 blocks and it costs $2 USD. At that time i thought, why not an app for this informal machine who really helps too much when you need to go short distances, 5 to 10 blocks.
