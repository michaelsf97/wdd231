How do i book a trycicle ride in my city?

To book a trycicle ride in your city, you must first create an account on our website. Once registered, you can log in and enter your location and destination to find available trycicle rides in your area. Nevertheless, you can select download app to book a ride on the go.

What are the safety measures for passengers?

Trycicle transportation offers several measures for passengers to ensure their safety during rides. These measures include:

  • Regular maintenance and inspections of trycicle machines to ensure they are in good working condition.
  • Providing safety instructions and guidelines to passengers before each ride.
  • Equipping trycicles with safety features such as seat belts and helmets for passengers.
  • Implementing a rating and feedback system for drivers to maintain high standards of service and safety.
